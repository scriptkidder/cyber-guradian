Protect Your Personal Information

Here are some tips to protect your personal information online:

  • Use strong, unique passwords for each account
  • Avoid sharing personal information on social media
  • Enable two-factor authentication whenever possible

Recognize Phishing Attempts

Learn how to recognize and avoid phishing attempts:

  • Check for suspicious email addresses and URLs
  • Avoid clicking on links or downloading attachments from unknown sources
  • Double-check the legitimacy of requests for personal or financial information

Report Suspicious Activity

If you suspect that you're a victim of cybercrime or encounter suspicious activity online, report it to the appropriate authorities:

  • Contact your local law enforcement agency
  • File a report with the Internet Crime Complaint Center (IC3)
  • Alert the relevant online service providers or platforms
